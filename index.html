Our Design Ethos



Eco Friendly Design

We ensure to use locally sourced components and eco-friendly materials wherever possible. Our partnership with Bcomp has provided us with bio-degradable bodywork and aero package for the full car.

Steel Chassis

By using a steel aerospace chassis, we benefit from the ability to make modifacations rather than remanufacture. Furthermore at the end of life, we are able to reuse and recycle some of the core components.

Electric Powertrain

Since 2016 we have implemented an all electric powertrain in our vehciles. The electric motors boast singinifant increase in power and torque over a conventional petrol engine.

About



  • Design

    Our team of trained engineers will first start by taking your concept through the initial design stage on CAD. We use advanced automotive modeling software on Altair to help define the initial framework and then refine the deisgn further.

  • Validate

    In the next step, our engineers will validate the designed components using finite element analysis (FEA). In doing so, they can ensure the compenets will be able to cope with the extreme loads experienced during wheel-to-wheel racing.

  • Manufacture

    Once we are confident with the design, we can then begin the manufacturing process. Our team of certified welders are some of the best in the industry. With a turnaround time of about 2 weeks, we are able to get you new and replacmenet components ready for your next race.
